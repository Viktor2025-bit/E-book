Refund Policy

BEMS Books sells digital ebooks. Because digital products can be accessed immediately after purchase, completed ebook orders are generally non-refundable.

If a customer is charged but does not receive access instructions, or if the wrong ebook is delivered, support will review the order and resolve the issue with replacement access or a refund where appropriate.

Refund requests should be sent to support@bemsbooks.com with the order reference and account email.
