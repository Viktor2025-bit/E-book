Privacy Policy

BEMS Books collects only the information needed to create accounts, process ebook orders, and provide customer support. This may include name, email address, login provider, cart activity, order details, and payment reference information.

Payment card details are handled by Paystack and are not stored by BEMS Books. We use customer information to confirm orders, provide ebook access, improve the store, and respond to support requests.

Customers may contact support@bemsbooks.com for account or privacy questions.
