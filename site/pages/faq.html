FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Are these physical books?

No. BEMS Books sells ebooks and digital reading resources. No shipping address is required for checkout.

How do I receive my ebook?

After successful payment, the order is recorded on your account and access instructions are sent to your email.

Can I login with Google?

Yes. Google login remains available when OAuth credentials are configured. Email and password login also works for a self-contained demo.

What payment method is supported?

The checkout is prepared for Paystack. If Paystack keys are not configured, the cart explains what is missing instead of failing silently.
